Common Social Engineering Attack Methods

Home What is Social Engineering Attack Methods Quiz
  • Phishing: Fake emails pretending to be from banks or services, containing malicious links.
  • Vishing: Phone calls from scammers pretending to be support agents or bank staff.
  • Smishing: Fraudulent SMS messages containing dangerous links or fake information.
  • Pretexting: Impersonating a trusted person (colleague, delivery, etc.) to get access to information.
